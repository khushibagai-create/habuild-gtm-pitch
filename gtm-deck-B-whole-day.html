How the WhatsApp activity works, and our rules

Members try it on WhatsApp, then open the app.

How we send it

  • To start: we send the messages with quick-reply buttons, by hand.
  • Later: it can reply on its own. Tap "Back" and you get the back clip and a link straight to that part of the app.
  • Every message is written in English with a Hinglish version.
  • Open the WhatsApp flow

Rules we follow

  • Tap, don't type. Just one emoji or a simple button.
  • No scores, no guilt. Replies are happy and optional.
  • The WhatsApp bit should feel complete. The app is more, not a wall.
